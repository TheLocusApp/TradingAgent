STRATEGY_NAME: DualMomentum

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy utilizes a dual exponential moving average crossover system specifically designed for the QQQ ETF on 5-minute timeframes, focusing on capturing short-term momentum shifts.

KEY STRATEGY COMPONENTS:
- Instrument: QQQ (Invesco QQQ Trust tracking NASDAQ-100)
- Timeframe: 5-minute charts exclusively
- Primary Indicators: 8-period EMA and 13-period EMA
- Core Concept: Capitalize on short-term momentum shifts when the faster EMA crosses the slower EMA

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
- Trigger when 8-period EMA crosses ABOVE the 13-period EMA
- Entry on the close of the candle where crossover occurs
- Confirm with price action showing bullish momentum

SHORT ENTRY:
- Trigger when 8-period EMA crosses BELOW the 13-period EMA  
- Entry on the close of the candle where crossover occurs
- Confirm with price action showing bearish momentum

EXIT RULES:
LONG POSITION EXIT:
- When 8-period EMA crosses below 13-period EMA
- Exit on the close of the crossover candle

SHORT POSITION EXIT:
- When 8-period EMA crosses above 13-period EMA
- Exit on the close of the crossover candle

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position sizing: Risk no more than 1-2% of account per trade
- Stop loss: Place 0.5-1% below/above recent swing low/high
- Maximum daily loss limit: 5% of account equity
- Avoid trading during first 30 minutes of market open due to volatility
- Consider reducing position size during high volatility periods

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. 8-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
2. 13-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
3. Optional: Volume confirmation for crossover validity

OPTIMIZATION NOTES:
- Best performance during regular market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM EST)
- Monitor for false signals during low-volume periods
- Consider filtering trades during major economic announcements
- Backtest optimal stop-loss levels for current market volatility conditions
