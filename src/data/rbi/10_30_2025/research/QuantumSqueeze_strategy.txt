STRATEGY_NAME: QuantumSqueeze

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy combines Bollinger Band squeeze detection with momentum confirmation on BTC daily timeframe, focusing on capturing explosive price movements after periods of low volatility compression.

KEY STRATEGY COMPONENTS:
1. Bollinger Bands (20,2) - Identifies volatility compression when bands squeeze
2. Keltner Channels (20,1.5) - Confirms squeeze when BBands are inside Keltner
3. RSI (14) - Momentum confirmation filter
4. Volume - Breakout validation
5. MACD (12,26,9) - Additional momentum confirmation

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
- Bollinger Bands width is at 6-month lows (squeeze condition)
- Price breaks above upper Bollinger Band
- RSI above 50 and rising
- Volume increases by 50%+ from 20-day average
- MACD histogram turns positive

SHORT ENTRY:
- Bollinger Bands width is at 6-month lows (squeeze condition)
- Price breaks below lower Bollinger Band
- RSI below 50 and falling
- Volume increases by 50%+ from 20-day average
- MACD histogram turns negative

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
- Price closes below middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA)
- RSI crosses below 70 (overbought)
- Trailing stop: 2 ATR below highest high since entry

SHORT EXIT:
- Price closes above middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA)
- RSI crosses above 30 (oversold)
- Trailing stop: 2 ATR above lowest low since entry

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position size: 1-2% of capital per trade
- Maximum 5% portfolio exposure to BTC
- Stop loss: 2 ATR from entry price
- No new entries if 3 consecutive losing trades
- Wait for squeeze to reset (BBands width returns to normal) before next trade

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
- Bollinger Bands (20,2)
- Keltner Channels (20,1.5)
- RSI (14)
- Volume (20-day average)
- MACD (12,26,9)
- ATR (14) for stop loss calculation

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS FOR BTC:
- Account for crypto market 24/7 operation (use UTC daily closes)
- Be aware of weekend volatility patterns
- Monitor Bitcoin dominance for broader market context
- Consider halving cycles and major news events that may affect volatility compression periods
