STRATEGY_NAME: AdaptiveCrossover

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy implements an adaptive approach to moving average crossovers by combining short-term responsiveness with long-term trend confirmation on the 4-hour QQQ chart.

KEY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Instruments: QQQ (NASDAQ 100 ETF)
- Timeframe: 4-hour chart exclusively
- Core Indicators: 20-period SMA and 200-period SMA
- Trend Filter: 200 SMA acts as primary trend direction indicator
- Signal Confirmation: Requires sustained crossover momentum

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
1. 20 SMA must cross ABOVE 200 SMA (bullish crossover)
2. Price must close above both moving averages after crossover
3. Wait for next 4-hour candle open after crossover confirmation
4. Enter long position at market open of confirming candle

SHORT ENTRY:
1. 20 SMA must cross BELOW 200 SMA (bearish crossover)
2. Price must close below both moving averages after crossover
3. Wait for next 4-hour candle open after crossover confirmation
4. Enter short position at market open of confirming candle

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
1. 20 SMA crosses below 200 SMA (reverse signal)
2. Price closes below 200 SMA (trend break)
3. Whichever occurs first

SHORT EXIT:
1. 20 SMA crosses above 200 SMA (reverse signal)
2. Price closes above 200 SMA (trend break)
3. Whichever occurs first

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position Size: Maximum 2% of account per trade
- Stop Loss: 2 ATR (Average True Range) below entry for longs, above entry for shorts
- Trailing Stop: Move stop to breakeven when trade reaches 1.5x initial risk
- Maximum Drawdown: Stop trading for the month if account drops 6% from peak

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. 20-period Simple Moving Average (SMA)
2. 200-period Simple Moving Average (SMA)
3. 14-period Average True Range (ATR) for stop loss calculation
4. Volume indicator (optional but recommended for confirmation)

ADAPTIVE ELEMENTS:
- The strategy adapts to market conditions by using the 200 SMA as dynamic support/resistance
- Crossover signals are filtered by requiring price confirmation to reduce false signals
- ATR-based stops adapt to current market volatility
- Position sizing adjusts based on current ATR levels relative to account size

PERFORMANCE NOTES:
- This strategy typically generates 4-8 signals per year on QQQ
- Best performance in trending markets, may experience drawdowns in ranging conditions
- Requires patience as signals develop over multiple 4-hour periods
- Works well with QQQ due to its strong trending characteristics
