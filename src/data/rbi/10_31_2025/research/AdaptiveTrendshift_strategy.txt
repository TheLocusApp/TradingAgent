STRATEGY_NAME: AdaptiveTrendshift

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy uses dual exponential moving averages on QQQ's 1-hour chart to identify significant trend changes and capture sustained directional moves while filtering out market noise.

KEY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Instrument: QQQ (NASDAQ 100 ETF)
- Timeframe: 1-hour chart
- Core Indicators: 20-period EMA (fast) and 200-period EMA (slow)
- Trend Filter: 200 EMA serves as primary trend direction indicator
- Signal Generator: 20 EMA crossover relative to 200 EMA

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
1. 20 EMA crosses ABOVE 200 EMA (bullish crossover)
2. Price confirms by closing above both EMAs
3. Enter on next candle open after crossover confirmation
4. Optional: Wait for pullback to 20 EMA for better risk-reward

SHORT ENTRY:
1. 20 EMA crosses BELOW 200 EMA (bearish crossover)
2. Price confirms by closing below both EMAs
3. Enter on next candle open after crossover confirmation
4. Optional: Wait for pullback to 20 EMA for better risk-reward

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
1. 20 EMA crosses below 200 EMA (reverse signal)
2. Price closes below 200 EMA (trend break)
3. Protective stop loss hit

SHORT EXIT:
1. 20 EMA crosses above 200 EMA (reverse signal)
2. Price closes above 200 EMA (trend break)
3. Protective stop loss hit

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position Size: 1-2% of account capital per trade
- Stop Loss: Place below 200 EMA for longs, above 200 EMA for shorts (adjust based on volatility)
- Alternative Stop: Recent swing low/high with buffer
- Take Profit: No fixed target - let trend run until exit signal
- Risk-Reward: Minimum 1:2 ratio required

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. 20-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
2. 200-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
3. Optional: Volume confirmation for crossover validity

STRATEGY NOTES:
- This strategy works best in trending markets, may produce whipsaws in ranging conditions
- The 200 EMA acts as major support/resistance and trend filter
- Consider market context and overall trend alignment for higher probability trades
- Monitor for false breakouts by waiting for candle close confirmation
- Best suited for swing trading with positions held for days to weeks
