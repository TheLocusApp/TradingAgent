STRATEGY_NAME: DivergenceConfirmation

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy combines RSI divergence patterns with MACD confirmation signals on Bitcoin's daily timeframe to identify high-probability reversal opportunities.

KEY COMPONENTS:
1. RSI (Relative Strength Index) - Set to 14 periods
2. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) - Standard settings (12,26,9)
3. Price action analysis for divergence identification
4. Daily timeframe for reliable signal generation

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
- RSI shows BULLISH DIVERGENCE (price makes lower low, RSI makes higher low)
- MACD histogram shows upward momentum (rising bars above zero line)
- MACD line crosses above signal line OR shows bullish crossover
- Entry on next candle open after all conditions are met

SHORT ENTRY:
- RSI shows BEARISH DIVERGENCE (price makes higher high, RSI makes lower high)
- MACD histogram shows downward momentum (falling bars below zero line)
- MACD line crosses below signal line OR shows bearish crossover
- Entry on next candle open after all conditions are met

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
- RSI reaches overbought zone (above 70)
- MACD shows bearish crossover
- Stop loss hit
- Take profit target reached (2:1 risk-reward ratio minimum)

SHORT EXIT:
- RSI reaches oversold zone (below 30)
- MACD shows bullish crossover
- Stop loss hit
- Take profit target reached (2:1 risk-reward ratio minimum)

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position size: Maximum 2% of capital per trade
- Stop loss: Place below recent swing low (long) or above recent swing high (short)
- Risk-reward ratio: Minimum 1:2
- Maximum consecutive losses: 3 trades before strategy review

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. RSI (14 periods) - For divergence detection
2. MACD (12,26,9) - For momentum confirmation
3. Price chart with swing point identification

ADDITIONAL NOTES:
- Wait for daily candle close before taking signals
- Multiple timeframe confirmation (4H) can improve accuracy
- Avoid trading during major news events
- Backtest divergence patterns for better pattern recognition
- Consider volume confirmation for stronger signals
