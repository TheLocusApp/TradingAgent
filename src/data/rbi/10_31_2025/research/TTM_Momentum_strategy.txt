STRATEGY_NAME: TTM_Momentum

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy utilizes the TTM (Trade The Momentum) indicator on QQQ daily charts to capture momentum-based trading opportunities in the technology-heavy ETF.

KEY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Instrument: QQQ (Invesco QQQ Trust)
- Timeframe: Daily charts
- Core Indicator: TTM Momentum (specifically the TTM Squeeze or TTM Wave)
- Market Focus: Technology sector momentum moves

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. TTM Momentum Indicator (typically shows momentum as colored histogram bars)
2. Optional: 20-period EMA for trend confirmation
3. Volume confirmation for momentum validation

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
- TTM Momentum histogram turns from red to green (positive momentum)
- Price above 20-period EMA for trend alignment
- Confirming volume spike on the momentum shift candle
- Entry on next candle open after momentum signal

SHORT ENTRY:
- TTM Momentum histogram turns from green to red (negative momentum)
- Price below 20-period EMA for trend alignment
- Confirming volume spike on the momentum shift
- Entry on next candle open after momentum signal

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
- TTM Momentum histogram turns from green to red
- Or price closes below 20-period EMA
- Or predetermined profit target reached (suggested: 3-5% for daily timeframe)

SHORT EXIT:
- TTM Momentum histogram turns from red to green
- Or price closes above 20-period EMA
- Or predetermined profit target reached

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position size: Maximum 2% of capital per trade
- Stop loss: 2% below entry for long positions, 2% above for short positions
- Maximum daily drawdown: 5% of account
- No new entries if 3 consecutive losing trades occur
- Avoid trading during major earnings announcements for QQQ components

ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS:
- Monitor overall market conditions (VIX, SPY correlation)
- QQQ tends to be more volatile than broader market - adjust position sizing accordingly
- Best performance during trending markets, may underperform during choppy/range-bound periods
- Consider using weekly charts for broader trend context before taking daily signals
