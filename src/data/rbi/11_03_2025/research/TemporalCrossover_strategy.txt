STRATEGY_NAME: TemporalCrossover

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy employs a dual-timeframe exponential moving average crossover system specifically designed for SPY hourly charts, focusing on the interaction between short-term and medium-term trend dynamics.

KEY STRATEGY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Instrument: SPY (SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust)
- Timeframe: Hourly charts exclusively
- Core Indicators: 20-period EMA (short-term) and 100-period EMA (medium-term)
- The 20 EMA represents recent price momentum and immediate trend direction
- The 100 EMA captures the broader medium-term trend structure
- Crossover events signal potential trend reversals or accelerations

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
- Wait for the 20 EMA to cross ABOVE the 100 EMA
- Enter on the next hourly candle following the confirmed crossover
- Confirm bullish momentum with the crossover candle closing above the 20 EMA
- Optional: Volume confirmation with above-average volume on crossover candle

SHORT ENTRY:
- Wait for the 20 EMA to cross BELOW the 100 EMA
- Enter on the next hourly candle following the confirmed crossover
- Confirm bearish momentum with the crossover candle closing below the 20 EMA
- Optional: Volume confirmation with above-average volume on crossover candle

EXIT RULES:
LONG POSITION EXIT:
- Exit when 20 EMA crosses below 100 EMA (reverse signal)
- Alternative: Use trailing stop of 2-3% below recent swing low
- Time-based exit after 5-7 days if no significant movement

SHORT POSITION EXIT:
- Exit when 20 EMA crosses above 100 EMA (reverse signal)
- Alternative: Use trailing stop of 2-3% above recent swing high
- Time-based exit after 5-7 days if no significant movement

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position sizing: Risk no more than 1-2% of capital per trade
- Stop loss placement: 2-3% below entry for longs, 2-3% above entry for shorts
- Maximum portfolio exposure: 5-10% across all positions
- Avoid trading during major economic announcements or market holidays
- Monitor for false breakouts by waiting for candle close confirmation

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. 20-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
2. 100-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
3. Optional: Volume indicator for confirmation
4. Optional: ATR (Average True Range) for dynamic stop loss calculation

ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS:
- This strategy works best in trending markets, may generate whipsaws in ranging conditions
- Consider filtering trades with broader market context (daily chart trend direction)
- Monitor for convergence/divergence between EMAs as early warning signals
- Backtest specific to SPY's characteristic volatility patterns and trading hours
