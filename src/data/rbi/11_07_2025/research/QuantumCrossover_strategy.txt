STRATEGY_NAME: QuantumCrossover

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy employs a precise dual-exponential moving average crossover system specifically designed for the QQQ ETF on 5-minute timeframes, focusing on capturing short-term momentum shifts with rapid execution.

KEY STRATEGY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Instrument: QQQ (Invesco QQQ Trust ETF)
- Timeframe: 5-minute charts exclusively
- Core Indicators: 8-period EMA and 13-period EMA
- Trading Style: Short-term momentum-based swing trading
- Market Focus: NASDAQ 100 technology-heavy index tracking

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
1. Wait for the 8-period EMA to cross ABOVE the 13-period EMA
2. Confirm bullish crossover with current price trading above both EMAs
3. Enter long position on the next candle following confirmed crossover

SHORT ENTRY:
1. Wait for the 8-period EMA to cross BELOW the 13-period EMA
2. Confirm bearish crossover with current price trading below both EMAs
3. Enter short position on the next candle following confirmed crossover

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
1. Close position when 8-period EMA crosses below 13-period EMA
2. Alternative exit: Set trailing stop at recent swing low or use fixed 0.5-1% stop

SHORT EXIT:
1. Close position when 8-period EMA crosses above 13-period EMA
2. Alternative exit: Set trailing stop at recent swing high or use fixed 0.5-1% stop

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position sizing: Risk no more than 1-2% of capital per trade
- Stop-loss placement: 0.5-1% below entry for longs, above entry for shorts
- Maximum daily loss limit: 3% of account equity
- Avoid trading during first/last 30 minutes of market hours due to volatility
- Filter out low-volume periods (lunch hours, pre-market, after-hours)

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. 8-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
2. 13-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
3. Volume indicator (for confirmation)
4. Optional: RSI (14-period) for overbought/oversold filtering

ADDITIONAL FILTERS:
- Only trade during high-volume periods (9:30 AM - 11:30 AM and 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM ET)
- Avoid trading during major economic announcements
- Confirm crossover with price action and volume increase
- Consider market context (overall trend on higher timeframes)

This strategy excels in capturing quick momentum shifts in the highly liquid QQQ, leveraging the sensitivity of the 8/13 EMA combination on 5-minute charts for rapid signal generation while maintaining disciplined risk parameters.
