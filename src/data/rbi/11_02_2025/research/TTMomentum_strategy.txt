STRATEGY_NAME: TTMomentum

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy focuses on using TTM Squeeze momentum indicators specifically for TSLA on hourly timeframes to capture momentum breakouts from consolidation periods.

KEY STRATEGY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Instrument: TSLA (Tesla) stock
- Timeframe: Hourly charts exclusively
- Core Indicator: TTM Squeeze system
- Focus: Momentum breakouts from squeeze conditions
- Market Condition: Works best during trending markets with periodic consolidation

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. TTM Squeeze Indicator (primary)
   - Shows squeeze conditions (dots between Bollinger Bands and Keltner Channels)
   - Momentum histogram bars (red/green)
2. Volume confirmation
3. Price action confirmation

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
1. Wait for TTM Squeeze to show compression (multiple dots between bands)
2. Look for first green momentum histogram bar after compression
3. Confirm with bullish price action (break above recent high)
4. Enter on next candle after confirmation
5. Volume should be increasing on breakout

SHORT ENTRY:
1. Wait for TTM Squeeze compression period
2. Look for first red momentum histogram bar after compression
3. Confirm with bearish price action (break below recent low)
4. Enter on next candle after confirmation
5. Volume should confirm the move

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
- When momentum histogram turns red
- When price closes below recent swing low
- When TTM Squeeze shows new compression forming
- Target: 2:1 risk-reward ratio minimum

SHORT EXIT:
- When momentum histogram turns green
- When price closes above recent swing high
- When TTM Squeeze shows new compression forming
- Target: 2:1 risk-reward ratio minimum

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position size: Maximum 2% of account per trade
- Stop loss: Place below recent swing low (long) or above recent swing high (short)
- Risk-reward: Minimum 1:2 ratio required
- Maximum daily trades: 3 to avoid overtrading
- Avoid trading during major TSLA news events or earnings

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS FOR TSLA:
- TSLA exhibits high volatility - adjust position sizing accordingly
- Monitor overall market sentiment as TSLA is momentum-sensitive
- Be aware of Elon Musk-related news that can cause sudden price movements
- Consider higher timeframes (4H/Daily) for overall trend context
