STRATEGY_NAME: AdaptiveCrossover

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy utilizes a dual exponential moving average (EMA) crossover system on SPY hourly charts, combining trend identification with momentum confirmation for precise entry and exit timing.

KEY STRATEGY COMPONENTS:
- Primary Trend Indicator: 200-period EMA (long-term trend direction)
- Signal Generator: 20-period EMA (short-term momentum)
- Asset: SPY (SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust)
- Timeframe: 1-hour charts
- Core Concept: Capitalize on significant trend changes while filtering out minor fluctuations

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
1. Wait for 20-period EMA to cross ABOVE 200-period EMA
2. Enter on the next candle after crossover confirmation
3. Confirm bullish momentum with rising price action
4. Optional: Wait for price to retest the 20 EMA as support after crossover

SHORT ENTRY:
1. Wait for 20-period EMA to cross BELOW 200-period EMA
2. Enter on the next candle after crossover confirmation
3. Confirm bearish momentum with declining price action
4. Optional: Wait for price to retest the 20 EMA as resistance after crossover

EXIT RULES:
LONG POSITIONS:
1. Exit when 20-period EMA crosses below 200-period EMA
2. Alternative: Use trailing stop based on recent swing lows or ATR
3. Consider partial profit-taking at key resistance levels

SHORT POSITIONS:
1. Exit when 20-period EMA crosses above 200-period EMA
2. Alternative: Use trailing stop based on recent swing highs or ATR
3. Consider partial profit-taking at key support levels

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Position sizing: Risk 1-2% of capital per trade
- Stop loss: Place below recent swing low (longs) or above recent swing high (shorts)
- Maximum drawdown: Limit to 5-8% of total capital
- Trade frequency: Avoid overtrading - wait for clear, confirmed crossovers
- Market hours: Focus on regular trading hours for better liquidity and volume

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. 20-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
2. 200-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
3. Optional: Volume confirmation for crossover validity
4. Optional: ATR (Average True Range) for stop loss placement

ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS:
- Monitor overall market conditions and SPY volume
- Be aware of economic events that may cause false signals
- Consider using longer timeframes (daily) for trend confirmation
- The strategy works best in trending markets, may underperform in ranging conditions
- Backtest specific parameters for SPY's unique volatility characteristics
