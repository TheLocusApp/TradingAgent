STRATEGY_NAME: VolatilityBreakout

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy capitalizes on periods of low volatility followed by high-momentum breakouts using Bollinger Bands on SPY 1-hour timeframe.

KEY COMPONENTS:
- Bollinger Bands (20-period, 2 standard deviations)
- Volume confirmation
- Squeeze identification
- Breakout momentum detection

REQUIRED INDICATORS:
1. Bollinger Bands (20,2)
2. Volume indicator
3. Optional: RSI (14) for momentum confirmation

ENTRY RULES:
LONG ENTRY:
1. Identify Bollinger Band squeeze (bands width < 2% of price)
2. Wait for price to break ABOVE upper Bollinger Band
3. Confirm with increased volume (at least 20% above 20-period average)
4. Enter on next candle opening after breakout confirmation

SHORT ENTRY:
1. Identify Bollinger Band squeeze (bands width < 2% of price)
2. Wait for price to break BELOW lower Bollinger Band
3. Confirm with increased volume (at least 20% above 20-period average)
4. Enter on next candle opening after breakout confirmation

EXIT RULES:
LONG EXIT:
- Take profit when price touches the middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA)
- Stop loss: 2% below entry price OR below the lower Bollinger Band
- Alternative: Exit when RSI reaches 70 (overbought)

SHORT EXIT:
- Take profit when price touches the middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA)
- Stop loss: 2% above entry price OR above the upper Bollinger Band
- Alternative: Exit when RSI reaches 30 (oversold)

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Maximum risk per trade: 1% of account
- Position sizing based on stop loss distance
- No new entries during last trading hour (3-4 PM EST)
- Avoid trading during major economic announcements

FILTERING CONDITIONS:
- Only trade between 9:30 AM - 3:00 PM EST
- Higher probability setups occur after extended consolidation (minimum 4 hours of squeeze)
- Avoid trading against the primary trend (use daily chart for context)

This strategy works best in ranging markets transitioning to trending conditions and requires patience to wait for genuine squeeze breakouts rather than false breakouts.
